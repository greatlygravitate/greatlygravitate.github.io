Atomic Structure

Atoms are the one of the smallest things (the smallest is a quark, a split proton and neutron). They make up everything. They are made up of protons, electrons, and neutrons. An atom looks like this:

This atom has 3 electrons (green), 3 protons (blue), and 4 neutrons (orange). The middle (protons and neutrons) is called the nucleus. Orbiting around it are the electrons. This is the basic structure of the atom.
In this course, you will learn the basics of atoms, and possibly, you may be able to draw one!

It is missing something. The neutrons.

Calculating Neutrons

This is also simple. Lets do some simple math.
The 10.811 is the Atomic Mass. Lets round it:
10.811 ≊ 11
Then we subtract the rounded number from the atomic mass.
11 - 5 = 6
So we have 6 neutrons!
